## Traffic & Travel Insights

### Peak Hours to Avoid
- **Morning Rush** - 8:00 AM to 10:30 AM
- **Evening Rush** - 5:30 PM to 9:00 PM (worst time)
- **Weekday vs Weekend** - Weekdays much worse than weekends

### Major Routes & Conditions
- **NH-24 (Delhi-Ghaziabad Highway)** - Usually crowded in evenings, watch for trucks
- **Delhi-Noida DND Flyway** - Peak traffic 6-8 PM
- **Ghaziabad Bypass** - Better alternative during rush hours
- **East Delhi to Noida** - Via Delhi-Noida border can be very slow
- **Sahibabad to Central Noida** - Via NH-24 extension, avoid after 6 PM

### Travel Tips
- Best travel time: 11 AM to 4 PM
- Use local cabs (Uber/Ola) for better route knowledge
- Avoid traveling during festivals (Diwali, Holi) - extreme congestion
- Weekends: Better traffic but markets very crowded
- Railway is best option for Delhi-Ghaziabad: takes 30-40 mins

## Cultural Context

### Local Customs
- People are direct and friendly
- Chai (tea) is a way of socializing
- Street vendors are trustworthy but bargain normally
- Football (soccer) is very popular in NCR
- Cricket is the national obsession
- Festivals like Diwali, Holi, Eid are celebrated by all

### Common Phrases
- "Kaise ho?" = How are you?
- "Sab theek hai?" = Is everything okay?
- "Accha, theek hai" = Okay, that's fine
- "Nahi, bhai" = No, friend
- "Pakka?" = Sure / Confirmed?
- "Bilkul!" = Absolutely!
